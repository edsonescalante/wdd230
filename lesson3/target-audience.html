Purpose

Besides providing the current weather to the public of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas, Weather Awareness - Pro Tips walks the extra mile by providing them helpful recommendations to be better prepared for unexpected changes in the weather. Our main purpose is to guide the public to become awared of the unexpected, and help them be prepared to stay safe out there.
