Our Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce of Boise has the purpose to boost the digital transformation of the businesses that are associated with the CCB. We provide information, training, events, and specialized services for the transformation your business and its digital evolution.

We achieve these goals with our innovation, technological tools, and strategic alliances.

HOT DOG

The Hot Dog is a specialized mobile food stand for preparing and selling street food, specifically hot dogs, to passersby. The hot dogs are often kept hot within a pan of hot water, and some refer to them as "dirty water dogs" per this method. An on-board cooler is used to keep the hot dogs safely chilled until ready for reheating. It also provides cold storage for beverages, such as sodas, and multiple sinks for washing and cleaning utensils.
